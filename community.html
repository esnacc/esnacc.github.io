eSNACC Community Collaboration

Mailing Lists

The Enhanced SNACC Mailing lists can all be found at http://mail.esnacc.org/mailman/listinfo. This page will merely explain the lists, and contain links to the archives.

  • dev - Mailing list where development for esnacc occurs (archives)
  • bugs - Mailing list for reporting and triaging bugs (archives)
  • discuss - Mailing list for general esnacc discussion, and announcements (archives)

IRC and Chat

eSNACC developers will sometimes hang out on irc.freenode.net/6667 on the #esnacc channel.
